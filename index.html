Short Bio

I am working toward my Ph.D. degree at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Shuai Ma. I received my BS degree also in computer science and technology from Beihang University in 2014. Sponsored by the China Scholarship Council, I was a visitting student at the Data Mining group at Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, supervised by Dr. Hui Xiong from September 2017 to April 2018. From May 2018 to September 2019, I had been a research intern at Business Intelligence Lab, Baidu Research. My research focuses on graph algorithms and representation learning for mobile analytics and business intelligence.
