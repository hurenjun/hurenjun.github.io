Short Bio

I obtained my bachelor and Ph.D. degrees at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University in 2014 and 2020, respectively, supervised by Prof. Shuai Ma. Sponsored by the China Scholarship Council, I visitted the Data Mining group at Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey from September 2017 to April 2018, working with Dr. Hui Xiong. From May 2018 to September 2019, I had been a research intern at Business Intelligence Lab, Baidu Research. Since July 2020, I have joined Alibaba Group as an algorithm engineer and mainly worked on modeling massive sparse and heterogeneou tabular data for online advertisement. My research focuses on graph and tabular data mining and robust and explainable machine learning for mobile analytics and business intelligence.
